Transportation

Bus Route Information

Buses on Taniti can take you to any of the islands' many incredible sights and experiences. Public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5am to 11pm every day.

Taxi Service Information

The Taniti Taxi Service runs 24/7 and is always available to get you wherever you need to go on the island. Anywhere.
